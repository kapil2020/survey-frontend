Impact of Air Pollution Exposure on Travel Behaviour

Did You Know? Our exposure to air pollution is highest during travel—more than any other daily activity! 🚗🚌🛵 From sitting in traffic jams 🚦 to waiting at bus stops ⏳, commuting exposes you to harmful pollutants at levels far greater than indoors or other activities. 🌍💡

Choose smarter routes, safer modes, and reduce the impact on your health. 🫁✨ Travel smart. Breathe healthy. 🌬️

📋 About the Survey: 🚦 How does air quality influence your travel choices? This survey dives into how real-time air quality information 🌬️ affects your decisions on routes and modes of transportation. Our aim is to uncover ways to help private vehicle users 🚗 shift towards healthier, greener options like public transit 🚌🚇.

💡 Your responses will pave the way for a smarter, more sustainable future—making cities healthier 🌿 and commutes safer for everyone! 🌍
